Prologue

Prologue: It All Started in High School Psychology

The Boisterous Jam Band Guy + The Quiet Studious Girl start to sit together often...

First Date, they get lost driving to a movie, distracted by each other. He woos her with Mario Kart and she says "Sure. Let's do this."

Then came physics. Loose-cannon teacher dedicates 8+ classes to analyzing this mysterious new pair. His conclusion? "They balance each other out. I think I get it."

Balance was indeed the key: He taught her how to loosen up, and she encouraged him to show up to class. He taught her to ski (and she threw mittens at his face for taking her down a black diamond too soon). She took him on his first run and to his first yoga class (and he was annoyingly good at both right away). From many drives between HWS and Ithaca, to living on Cup Noodles in a van traveling around New Zealand, to exploring British Columbia with 15 wild roommates, to moving home to their beloved Boston, life side-by-side has been the best kind of fun.

To all of our friends and family: Thank you for joining us as we celebrate the next big step and many adventures ahead!

Love, Brittany & Chris
